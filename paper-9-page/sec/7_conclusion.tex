\section{Conclusion}

The pilot across 50 scenes demonstrates that structured, validity-audited single-lever edits can be generated at scale and scored by an auxiliary proxy, producing preliminary directional patterns -- Mobility Infrastructure and Physical Maintenance yield the most consistent positive shifts -- that prioritise where human evaluation should focus. More broadly, the framework shifts the explanatory unit from post-hoc pixel attribution to semantically grounded interventions whose validity is auditable before any perceptual claim is made. Realising that potential requires planner triage, stronger editing control, critic calibration, and ultimately pairwise human judgement as the ground-truth endpoint.
