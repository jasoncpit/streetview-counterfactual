\section{Conclusion}

We have presented an interventional attribution framework for street-view
perception that moves beyond correlational feature importance toward
testable, per-scene hypotheses. By constructing a deterministic
scene-specific candidate set, realizing constrained prompt-only edits,
and filtering them through a validity audit, we operationalise the
multi-optima question under single-lever intervention testing. The
current pilot stage provides validity and auxiliary scoring diagnostics,
while randomized human pairwise evaluation defines the primary endpoint
for the observed effective lever set. We expect this framework to be of
interest to the XAI for Computer Vision community, particularly in the
context of human-grounded and counterfactual explanation methods.
